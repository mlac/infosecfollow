   prompt-injection, vulnerability]
   Latest developments: Obsidian Security disclosed a three-bug

   to full admin and run code, exposing every model-provider key
   the gateway holds, while separate research showed that a
   13-word snippet planted on Reddit, Wikipedia, or Quora can
